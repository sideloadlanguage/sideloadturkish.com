Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

What data we collect

By default, none. Sideload Turkish does not collect, sell, or share personal data for analytics, advertising, or tracking. Your learning progress and settings are stored locally in your browser.

What stays on your device

  • Your learning progress, including words known and seen counts
  • Your settings, including density, blacklist, and toggle state
  • The bundled vocabulary list used by the extension

Optional sync

The extension includes an optional sync capability that is not currently being sold on the public site. If sync is enabled in a future launch, progress data would be encrypted on your device before upload so the server cannot read its contents.

Network requests and analytics

The extension does not use analytics, ads, or telemetry. It only makes network requests if the optional sync feature is enabled for backup or restore.

Waitlist

The public website may offer a simple mailing-list or waitlist contact flow to measure interest in sync. That flow is separate from the extension itself.

Third-party services

  • Sideload Turkish sync service — stores only encrypted sync blobs if sync is enabled in a future launch.
  • Buttondown — processes mailing-list and waitlist subscriptions submitted through the public website.

Contact

Questions? Open an issue on GitHub.

Changes

We will update this policy if the extension's data practices change. Any expanded sync or network behavior will require updated disclosure.
